Edit Categories




Your website is sorted by categories. The more categories you have, the more pages you have at your disposal to sort your website.

Deleting a category will delete all posts inside it.

Renaming a category will move all posts to the new category.

If you are renaming a category with multiple posts, it make take some time. Try not to shut down the tab after submitting the rename.
